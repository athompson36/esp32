Backup / Restore / Flash

All flashing is internal (this app, esptool) and launcher/firmware compatible based on your selection. Backup to .bin, restore from .bin, or flash a build. Choose a Firmware target to filter artifacts (Meshtastic, MeshCore, Launcher). Click Refresh & detect devices to auto-detect connected ESP32 boards (port and chip). Device must be connected via USB; esptool must be installed on the host.

SD card: devices with an SD slot (e.g. T-Beam) are not backed up or restored through this interface. Remove the card and use host tools (e.g. disk image / dd) to image or restore the SD.
