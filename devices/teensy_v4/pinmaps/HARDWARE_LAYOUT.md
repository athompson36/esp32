# Teensy v4.1 — Hardware Layout

**Boards:** PJRC Teensy 4.0, Teensy 4.1  
**MCU:** NXP i.MX RT1062 (Cortex-M7, 600–912 MHz)

---

## Pinout

- **Teensy 4.0:** 40 digital I/O; 14 analog inputs; first 28 pins in 2×14 grid (compatible with Teensy 3.x layout).
- **Teensy 4.1:** 55 digital I/O; 18 analog inputs; extra pins on ends and bottom.
- **Voltage:** **3.3 V only**; pins are **not 5 V tolerant** (unlike Teensy 3.x). Do not apply 5 V.

---

## Power

- 3.3 V from onboard regulator; USB or VIN. 3.3 V and GND on header.
